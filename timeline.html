Avengers Timeline

Prehistoric Era — Eternals

Celestials send the Eternals to Earth to protect humanity from Deviants.

1000s AD — Thor & Loki Origins

Odin defeats the Frost Giants; Loki is adopted after the war.

1943 — Captain America: The First Avenger

Steve Rogers becomes a super soldier, fights Red Skull, then is frozen.

1945 — Agent Carter Era

Peggy Carter helps found the foundation of S.H.I.E.L.D.

1950s–1970s — Ant-Man Legacy

Hank Pym operates as Ant-Man; Janet van Dyne is the Wasp.

1980s — Guardians of the Galaxy Setup

Ego plants his seed; Peter Quill abducted in 1988.

1989 — Janet van Dyne Disappears

Janet van Dyne shrinks into the Quantum Realm while disarming a missile.

1991 — Tony Stark’s Parents Killed by Winter Soldier

Bucky Barnes assassinates Howard and Maria Stark under Hydra’s control.

1995 — Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers gains powers and helps end the Kree-Skrull war.

2008 — Iron Man

Tony Stark builds the first Iron Man suit after captivity.

2010 — Iron Man 2

Stark faces enemies and government pressure.

2011 — Thor

Thor is banished and learns humility.

2012 — The Avengers

The team assembles to stop Loki and the Chitauri invasion.

2013 — Iron Man 3

Tony fights the Mandarin and PTSD.

2013 — Thor: The Dark World

Thor fights Malekith and the Dark Elves.

2014 — Captain America: The Winter Soldier

Steve discovers Hydra in S.H.I.E.L.D.; Bucky resurfaces.

2014 — Guardians of the Galaxy

Star-Lord and team stop Ronan the Accuser.

2015 — Avengers: Age of Ultron

Ultron created; Sokovia destroyed; Vision & Wanda introduced.

2016 — Captain America: Civil War

Avengers split over Sokovia Accords.

2016 — Black Panther

T’Challa becomes king and stops Killmonger.

2016 — Spider-Man: Homecoming

Peter Parker becomes a hero under Tony’s mentorship.

2021 — Black Widow

Natasha confronts her past and the Red Room.

2017 — Thor: Ragnarok

Thor loses hammer; Asgard destroyed; Hela defeated.

2018 — Avengers: Infinity War

Thanos collects Infinity Stones and snaps away half of life.

2018 — Ant-Man and the Wasp

Explores Quantum Realm and introduces Hope Van Dyne as Wasp.

2019 — Avengers: Endgame

Avengers reverse the Snap using time travel. Tony dies. Steve retires.

2023 — WandaVision

Wanda creates Westview Hex; becomes Scarlet Witch.

2023 — The Falcon and the Winter Soldier

Sam Wilson becomes new Captain America.

2023 — Spider-Man: Far From Home

Peter faces Mysterio post-Endgame.

2024 — Loki (Season 1)

Loki variant breaks timeline; meets TVA and Kang variant.

2024 — What If...? (Season 1)

Explores alternate MCU realities.

2024 — Hawkeye

Clint Barton trains Kate Bishop; confronts Ronin’s past.

2024 — Moon Knight

Marc Spector battles supernatural forces with dissociative identity disorder.

2024 — Ms. Marvel

Kamala Khan gains powers and discovers mutant heritage.

2024 — She-Hulk

Jennifer Walters becomes a Hulk; handles superhero law.

2025 — Secret Invasion

Skrulls infiltrate Earth; Nick Fury and Talos investigate.

2025 — Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

Rocket’s backstory revealed; Guardians disband.

2025 — The Marvels

Carol Danvers, Monica Rambeau, and Kamala Khan team up.
